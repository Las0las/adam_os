# Data Requirements (by pack)
- **Recruiting**: jobs, candidates, resumes/submission evidence.
- **Onboarding**: onboarding cases, tasks, owner assignments.
- **Support**: tickets, knowledge documents.
- **Claims**: claims, emails, attachments.
- **Executive/Commercial**: accounts, opportunities, contracts/invoices, risk evidence.
- **Healthcare Ops**: patient cases, referrals, authorizations.
- **Professional Services**: engagements, timesheets, invoices.
Evidence text is chunked + indexed so AI answers are grounded with citations.
